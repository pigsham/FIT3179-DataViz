Tourism Revenue by Countries (2010—2020)

As you slide through the years, the color intensity of green on the choropleth map decreases in certain countries. This signifies the decline in tourism revenue, especially in 2020 when the COVID-19 pandemic caused a significant drop in global tourism revenue.

Tourist Arrivals by Region (1995—2022)

Highest Tourist Arrivals: The region with the highest tourist arrivals is Africa, showing consistent growth over the years. By contrast, South Asia has seen the lowest tourist arrivals compared to other regions.

Significant Decline in 2020: Tourist arrivals dropped sharply across all regions in 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. This is particularly evident in Africa, Americas, East Asia, and Europe.
