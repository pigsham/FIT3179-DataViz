Explanation

Tourism Revenue by Countries (2010—2020)

As you slide through the years, the color intensity of green on the choropleth map decreases in certain countries. This signifies the decline in tourism revenue, especially in 2020 when the COVID-19 pandemic caused a significant drop in global tourism revenue.

Tourist Arrivals by Region (1995—2022)

The region with the highest tourist arrivals is Africa, showing consistent growth over the years. By contrast, South Asia has seen the lowest tourist arrivals compared to other regions.

Tourist arrivals dropped sharply across all regions in 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. This is particularly evident in Africa, Americas, East Asia, and Europe.

Travel Benefits

Travel offers numerous benefits for both mind and body. It broadens horizons by exposing you to new cultures and experiences, fostering open-mindedness and personal growth. Traveling enhances creativity, mental well-being, and happiness while promoting adaptability, breaking routine, and building confidence.

It deepens social connections, strengthens communication, and reduces stress. Beyond personal development, it boosts cognitive flexibility and provides a sense of adventure. Whether trying new foods, learning languages, or escaping routine, travel enriches mental health, physical well-being, and leaves lasting memories.

Travel Budget Plan Suggestions

Self Trip

For solo travelers, most of the budget goes to accommodation and transportation for comfort and mobility. Entertainment and activities are prioritized for exploration, while dining and shopping take a smaller share, reflecting modest personal spending.

Business Trip

Business trips focus on transportation and accommodation, with companies usually covering these costs. Food and entertainment are secondary, but some budget is allocated for business-related shopping or souvenirs.

Family Trip

Family trips prioritize accommodation and transportation for comfort. Food and dining are key for shared meals, while entertainment and shopping, mostly for souvenirs, have smaller budgets.
