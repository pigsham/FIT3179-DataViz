This map highlights global air pollution deaths per 100,000 people. In 2023, African countries show significantly higher death rates, while Asia sees moderate figures, and North America experiences lower numbers. Historically, global air pollution deaths were extremely high, but experienced a global decline leading to 2023. The drop in pollution deaths can be linked to global policies aimed at improving air quality and reducing industrial emissions.
